# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 25, 2026**

---

## 1. Overview

**One Billion Dollar Donation** (the "Site") is an individually operated site publishing a personal investment record. This policy describes information that may be processed under the Site's current code and operating practices.

---

## 2. Google Analytics and cookies

The Site has no registration, comments, checkout, or contact form and does not directly ask visitors to enter a name or phone number.

The Site uses **Google Analytics 4 (GA4, measurement ID `G-Z7Y0N822TN`)** to understand aggregate traffic, learn which content is used, and improve the Site. The analytics tag loads after the main content so that it does not block the initial page display.

Depending on the settings and visiting environment, Google may process the page visited, referring page, approximate location, browser and device information, page interactions, and cookies or similar identifiers. An IP address may be transmitted to Google as part of the network request. The operator does not receive a visitor's individual IP address or name in Analytics reports and does not combine Analytics data with other information to identify an individual.

- [How Google uses information from partner sites](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites?hl=en)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en)
- [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en)

Visitors can block or delete cookies through their browser settings. If analytics or advertising use is expanded for a region where prior consent is required, the Site will implement Google consent mode together with an appropriate consent-management mechanism.

## 3. Google AdSense connection status

The Site publishes a `google-adsense-account` meta tag and `ads.txt` for AdSense ownership verification. **No AdSense advertising runtime or ad placement is currently active.** At this stage, the Site's code does not make AdSense ad requests or display ads.

Before ads are activated, this policy will be updated to reflect the actual use of cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, and visitor choices by Google and any other third party. A Google-certified consent management platform (CMP) will be prepared before serving ads in regions where Google requires one, including the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland. Personalized advertising can be managed through [Google My Ad Center](https://myadcenter.google.com/).

GitHub's servers may systematically collect access logs (IP address, browser type, access time, etc.) to provide stable services, and this is handled in accordance with GitHub's own privacy policy.

- GitHub Privacy Policy: [https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
