# Copyright & Content Use

Unless otherwise identified, the articles, original diagrams, explanatory structure, and edited datasets on this site are created or compiled by the operator. Rights in third-party material and images remain with their respective owners and follow the terms of the credited source.

## Uses that are welcome

- Sharing a **link** to the original page
- Brief quotation with clear attribution and a link
- Reading and taking notes for personal study

## Uses that require prior permission

- Reproducing all or a substantial portion of an article or table in another site, newsletter, video, or dataset
- Republishing automatically collected content, a public translation, or commercial reuse
- Reposting an original image, chart, or data arrangement without attribution

For permission, use the [contact page](/en/contact) and identify the page, amount, medium, and purpose.

## Selection and copying restrictions

Text selection and ordinary copying are limited in the article body as an operating measure intended to discourage unauthorized reproduction. Links, tables, and code remain selectable where practical. This technical restriction does not expand copyright or determine whether a limited quotation is lawful.
