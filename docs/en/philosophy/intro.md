# 🪐 Introduction: A Horizon of 100 Years, and One Billion Dollars

> **"What is overwhelmingly more important than an investment strategy is an unwavering mindset, and at the pinnacle of that mindset lies 'Stay the Course'."**

This space is not merely an ordinary investment journal recording how to increase my account balance.

In the past, I was an ordinary retail investor who checked my account every day. I was like a frog in the well, becoming arrogant as if I owned the world over millions of won in profit, and wasting my emotions day by day, losing sleep over tens of millions of won in losses. Swept back and forth by the small waves of the market, I had even forgotten the fundamental reason why I was riding this agonizing roller coaster.

Then one day, the news of **"Warren Buffett's One Billion Dollar (about 1.3 trillion won) Donation"** struck me like a massive jolt and brought profound, shameful reflection. At that moment, I decided to completely rewrite the destination of my investments.

Money can never be the ultimate goal of investment. The accumulation of wealth should merely be a **process** to contribute to the wider world and our neighbors.

---

## ⏳ Extending the Investment Horizon to 100 Years

Most people set their investment timeline to 3 years, 5 years, or at most 10 to 20 years until retirement. However, investing with retirement as the goal inevitably breeds impatience. The pressure to 'quickly reach the target amount' inevitably invites the temptation of leverage and short-term trading, and that temptation leads to the most fatal mistakes.

Therefore, like Warren Buffett, **assuming that I will live to be 100 years old,** I expanded my investment horizon to a distant future.

I designed not a short-term 10-year compounding, but a massive snowball that will roll for a lifetime beyond half a century. Miraculously, the moment I extended the timeline to 100 years, all impatience disappeared. Even if the market were to crash by 20% tomorrow, I came to understand with my heart, not just my head, that it is merely a passing 'puddle' in the remaining 60-year journey.

The destination I aim to reach at the end of this epic time is clear. It is a massive contribution to the world that transcends personal glory, namely a **'One Billion Dollar Donation'**.

## 🏛️ Why Does Mindset Dominate Everything?

Through this site, I will reveal all the secrets of the unique 'Core & Satellite Solar System Portfolio' I have built, the 'KCA Strategy'—a Korean Won cost averaging method—and the 'Automated System' that perfectly controls them.

But be warned in advance: no matter how sophisticated and perfect an investment formula you get your hands on, in the end, **the one most likely to lead your investment to failure is yourself**.

When the market is bleeding and the media shouts of economic collapse, if you succumb to fear and press the sell button or stop your automatic transfers, any excellent strategy instantly becomes a piece of trash. Ninety percent of investment success or failure depends not on intelligence or mathematical formulas, but on **a strong spirit (mindset) that endures the noise and emotions of the market**.

How I came to build a mechanical system that completely excludes emotions through numerous experiments of failure and wandering, and what powerful psychological defense mechanisms allowed me to endure during market crashes... I will now begin to tell all those stories.
