# 🔍 4. ETF Selection & Tools Guide (Selection & Tools)

> "Even with a great investment strategy and automated system, if the vessel (asset) holding it is poor, it ultimately becomes nothing but a sandcastle."

I am sharing my 4 major criteria for selecting top-tier ETFs and stocks that reliably guarantee long-term upward trends and compound growth, along with a list of analysis sites/apps I use daily to efficiently research and backtest them.

---

## 🏗️ 4 Principles for Choosing a Good ETF

There are thousands of ETFs listed on the US stock market. The clear filters I use to weed out the noise and find quality products that can be safely and mechanically accumulated for over 10 years are as follows:

### 1. Asset Under Management (AUM) & Liquidity
* **Criteria**: The Asset Under Management (AUM) should be **at least $1B (about 1.3 trillion KRW)**, with abundant average daily trading volume.
* **Reason**: Small-scale ETFs with low trading activity incur unfavorable transaction costs (Slippage) due to the bid-ask spread when buying and selling. In extreme cases, the fund may face the worst-case scenario of liquidation (delisting), so I primarily enter large-cap assets.

### 2. Expense Ratio
* **Criteria**: For general index (passive) ETFs, aim for an **annual fee of 0.1% or less**, and for quant strategies like momentum, prefer **0.2% or less**.
* **Reason**: In the compounding snowball rolling over 10 or 20 years, fees are the most terrifying cancer cells that erode long-term returns.

### 3. Issuer Reliability (Issuer)
* **Criteria**: Prioritize products from the world's top-tier asset managers: **Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street, and Invesco**.
* **Reason**: Through economies of scale in business, they lower fees to the marginal level, and the risk of the asset manager's bankruptcy or a bank run is virtually zero.

### 4. Tracking Error & Premium/Discount
* **Criteria**: The tracking error should be **0.05%~0.1% or less**, and there should be almost no discrepancy between the Net Asset Value (NAV) and the actual trading price.
* **Reason**: This is an essential quality indicator of the fund, showing whether the ETF is faithfully tracking the underlying asset index it is supposed to replicate without error.

## 🛠️ Investment Analysis Tools & Websites I Utilize

> ⚠️ **"You told me to delete the apps, so do I have to look at all these tools every day?"**
>
> It means becoming insensitive to price fluctuations and sensational news, not to neglect studying the economy. One of the core tenets of the Bogleheads philosophy is **'Educate Yourself'**. 
> To avoid being swayed by the media's fear mongering or rumors, you need to know how to look directly at **'Raw Data'**, not news processed by someone else. The tools below are not for tracking prices. They are the 'intellectual arsenal' I have carefully selected and use every day to see through the essence of the market and fact-check.

### 📊 A. [Raw Data] Where to see facts instead of news
To avoid being shaken by the media's fuss, I directly check objective figures and crowd psychology.

* **FRED ([fred.stlouisfed.org](https://fred.stlouisfed.org))**:
  * **Core Use**: A vast official economic statistics database provided by the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis. Used to check 'real' indicators, not articles.
  * **Application**: Browsing macroeconomic raw data such as the history of the KRW-USD exchange rate, US base interest rate, money supply (M2), and the yield curve spread.
* **CME FedWatch Tool ([cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html))**:
  * **Core Use**: The best tool to avoid being swayed by rumors of the Fed's interest rate hikes/cuts.
  * **Application**: Based on federal funds futures price data, checking the market's 'real betting probability' on how interest rates will change at the next FOMC meeting.
* **Fear & Greed Index ([edition.cnn.com/markets/fear-and-greed](https://edition.cnn.com/markets/fear-and-greed))**:
  * **Core Use**: Taking a step back to observe the crowd psychology of the stock market.
  * **Application**: Intuitively checking with a score whether the market is falling into Extreme Fear or Extreme Greed.
* **Investing.com ([kr.investing.com](https://kr.investing.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: Real-time global economic calendar monitoring.

### 🛡️ B. [Backtest] Where to build unshakable conviction
Verifying how the planet (Core) I chose has weathered past crises to strengthen the mentality for long-term holding.

* **Portfolio Visualizer ([portfoliovisualizer.com](https://www.portfoliovisualizer.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: The ultimate tool for historical simulation.
  * **Application**: Gaining conviction by backtesting how my asset allocation ratio defended during past economic crises (MDD).
* **Macrotrends ([macrotrends.net](https://www.macrotrends.net))**:
  * **Core Use**: Visualizing 10-20 years of long-term financial data for specific assets.
  * **Application**: Surveying the long history of companies and ETFs that have endured major economic cycles, not just short-term 1-2 years.
* **ETF.com ([etf.com](https://www.etf.com)) / ETFdb ([etfdb.com](https://etfdb.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: In-depth dissection of global ETFs.
  * **Application**: Understanding stock overlap between ETFs, comparing fees, and tracking fund inflow/outflow trends.

### 🔬 C. [In-depth Research] Where to check with my own eyes, not others' words
Discovering my own Satellite assets and fact-checking, rather than relying on someone's stock recommendations.

* **FnGuide Company Guide ([comp.fnguide.com](http://comp.fnguide.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: The standard for Korean stock and domestic company analysis. Checking 10-year financial statements and consensus.
* **Seeking Alpha ([seekingalpha.com](https://seekingalpha.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: Checking in-depth analysis reports on global individual stocks/ETFs and original transcripts of Earnings Calls.
* **GuruFocus ([gurufocus.com](https://www.gurufocus.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: Checking corporate financial health such as the Piotroski F-Score and tracking the portfolios of global gurus (like Warren Buffett).
* **Finviz ([finviz.com](https://finviz.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: A heatmap to see the overall market flow at a glance and stock screening through numerous condition filterings.
* **TipRanks ([tipranks.com](https://www.tipranks.com))**:
  * **Core Use**: Final fundamental verification by reverse-engineering the trading trends of management (insiders) and the fund movements of institutions/hedge funds.
* **TradingView ([tradingview.com](https://www.tradingview.com)) / ETF Check ([etfcheck.co.kr](https://www.etfcheck.co.kr))**:
  * **Core Use**: Technical chart analysis, utilizing custom indicators, and detailed screening of domestically listed ETFs.

### 🤝 D. [Mindset Defense] Solidarity of Philosophy (Reddit Communities)
A psychological haven to steadfastly maintain principles without wavering during the lonely process of long-term investing.

* **r/Bogleheads ([reddit.com/r/Bogleheads](https://www.reddit.com/r/Bogleheads))**: 
  * The mecca for long-term passive index investors who share John Bogle's philosophy. Encouraging each other with "Don't look at the prices, live your daily life."
* **r/dividends ([reddit.com/r/dividends](https://www.reddit.com/r/dividends))**: 
  * A forum discussing dividend growth and cash flow investing, such as SCHD.
* **r/investing ([reddit.com/r/investing](https://www.reddit.com/r/investing))**: 
  * Comprehensive discussions on global macroeconomic news, corporate earnings analysis, and the overall asset class, along with monitoring various investment perspectives.
* **r/wallstreetbets ([reddit.com/r/wallstreetbets](https://www.reddit.com/r/wallstreetbets))**: 
  * An aggressive, individual stock-oriented channel focused on high-risk, high-reward. Used to monitor short-term speculative Meme Stocks trends from a step back as a contrarian indicator.
